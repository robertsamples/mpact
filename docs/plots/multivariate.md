# Multivariate Analysis (NMDS)

Nonmetric multidimensional scaling (NMDS) reduces the high-dimensional
metabolomics dataset to a low-dimensional view that's easy to interpret
visually. Samples are plotted by biological group, with 95% confidence
ellipses shown per group — samples that are closer together are more
similar in overall metabolome.

By default, technical replicates are averaged so NMDS runs at the
sample level. To instead evaluate clustering of individual technical
replicates, uncheck replicate averaging in the plot options dialog
(the small square button in the plot's toolbar).
